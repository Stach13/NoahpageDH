About Me

Hello! My name is Noah, and this is my Web Portfolio. I'm a passionate, determined, and charismatic content creator, who likes turning visions into reality. I'm obsessed with any form of multimedia that contains motion, as well as still motion (like photography). I'm also skillful in many aspects of web development, and UX design. I'm looking forward to continue my path in video creation, motion graphics, animation, and other media. I want to be clear that everything I created was used with creative software (like Adobe) and coding tools, I'm strictly against the use of AI for any creative design. Hoping it will lead to greater and better things along the way!
